Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our repair process? Find quick answers below.

How long do repairs take?
Most repairs are completed within 1–3 business days. Simple fixes like screen or battery replacements may be done the same day.
Do you offer warranties?
Yes. All repairs include a minimum 30-day warranty covering parts and labor.
Do I need an appointment?
Walk-ins are welcome, but scheduling ahead ensures faster service.
What devices do you repair?
We repair smartphones, laptops, tablets, and gaming consoles from most major brands.
