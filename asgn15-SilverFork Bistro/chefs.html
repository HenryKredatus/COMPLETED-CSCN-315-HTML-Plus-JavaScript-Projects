Meet Our Chefs

Chef Jarquanzela

Executive Chef

Trained in Paris and inspired by seasonal ingredients, Chef Jarquanzela brings a refined European touch to every dish. His passion lies in balancing elegance with bold, memorable flavors.

Chef Gordon Ramsey

Sous Chef

With roots in Latin cuisine, Gordon fuses traditional techniques with modern creativity. His dishes are known for vibrant flavors and artistic presentation.

Chef Jeffery Garbanzo

Pastry Chef

Jeffery specializes in delicate desserts that blend precision and beauty. From rich chocolate creations to light, airy pastries, his work is both indulgent and visually stunning.

Chef Hatchet

Grill & Specials Chef

Hatchet is known for his mastery of fire and flavor. His expertly grilled dishes and rotating specials highlight bold spices and locally sourced ingredients.
